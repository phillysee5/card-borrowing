How does the borrowing system work?

How long can I borrow an item for?

The standard borrowing period is 2 weeks, the specific dates will be emailed to you when you borrow an item. Any deviations to this lending period will have to be negotiated between the two parties. Because this is a community based system, we rely on the honesty and communication between community members to make it work.

Is there a limit to the amount of items I can borrow?

Yes currently you can only borrow two items at one time, unless your profile has risen to level 10 or above - in which case you can borrow up to 5.s

How does the experience system work?

Every time you borrow or lend an item you will receive experience. This will be awarded once the transaction is complete. For borrowing an item you will receive 100xp and for lending an item you will receive 300xp. The system is intended to encourage interaction and honesty between community members. As your experince increases you will unlock new items to display on your profile. These images have been sourced from talented artists in the community.

How does the flame token reward system work?

As you lend and borrow items you will earn both XP and flame tokens. For each borrow you will earn 1 flame token, and for each lend you will earn 3 flame tokens.

What are the rewards I can get with flame tokens?

These will vary, as they mostly comprise of rare and promotional cards that our suppliers give to us. Please check in store, or give the Cerberus team a call to find out what we currently have.
